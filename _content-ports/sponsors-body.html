Your Company Should Sponsor MakeHaven!

MakeHaven is a hub for creative and innovative people in the New Haven region. Sponsorship gives your company visibility and esteem within a community of thousands of visitors, event participants, and members who actively engage with MakeHaven.

Sponsorship also helps MakeHaven develop additional programming that strengthens the innovation ecosystem. Sponsorship is a tax-deductible donation.
