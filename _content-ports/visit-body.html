Getting Here

Parking
  • Street Metered Parking
    Metered spots directly in front and around the block on Chapel St.
  • 270 State St Garage — Discounted
    Discounted parking at $1.50/hour with stickers donated by MakeHaven (ask at the front desk).
  • Temple St Free Lots (evenings)
    Free lots between Wall & Elm St, Temple St — free from 4pm to 7am.
  • 360 State St Garage
    Up to 1 hour free with an Elm City Market purchase.
Public Transit

Multiple CTTransit bus lines stop on or near Chapel Street. MakeHaven is walkable from Union Station (Metro-North) — approximately 12 minutes on foot.

Biking

Indoor bike storage is available under the stairs — bring your own lock. MakeHaven is on New Haven's bike route network.

Accessibility
  • Elevator access to all floors
  • ADA-compliant doorways and walkways
  • Restrooms with hand rails
  • Ground-level entry at Chapel St

Need to report an accessibility issue? Let us know.
