Getting Started

Sign up online, complete your member profile, sign the waiver, watch the new member orientation video, pass the safety quiz, then attend an in-person orientation at MakeHaven. Once complete, you'll receive your keycard for 24/7 access.

No experience is required. MakeHaven welcomes complete beginners as well as seasoned makers. We have orientations and volunteer facilitators to help you learn whatever you want to make.

Absolutely! We run free Open Tours throughout the month. It's the best way to see the space, meet members, and decide if MakeHaven is right for you.

Minors are welcome to accompany a parent or guardian but cannot independently operate equipment that requires a safety badge.

Tools & Badges

Badge-required equipment needs a safety orientation before use. You can earn badges through our Build & Badge classes, or schedule a one-on-one checkout with a volunteer facilitator. Orientations typically take 15–90 minutes.

Watch the tool-specific safety video → pass the online safety quiz → complete a hands-on checkout with a certified facilitator. Once cleared, the badge is added to your member profile.

Yes, one-on-one orientations with volunteer facilitators are free for members. Build & Badge classes have a small fee to cover instructor time and materials.

Yes, members supply their own materials. MakeHaven's store offers convenience items like vinyl, filament, and common consumables.

Membership & Pricing

Standard membership is $62/month, billed monthly and auto-renewing. We also offer sliding-scale memberships from $30–$75/month for those with limited incomes, and a household plan for two adults at the same address.

Yes. Scholarships are available for those experiencing financial hardship. We prioritize people of color, women, and veterans. Contact us to apply.

Yes. Members can suspend their membership for up to 3 months. Suspension must be requested in advance.

Membership is month-to-month and can be cancelled with 30 days notice. There are no long-term contracts.

Access & Parking

Yes! All members receive a keycard that grants access to the space around the clock, every day of the year — including holidays. Some equipment requires a facilitator to be present.

Metered street parking is available on Chapel Street. Discounted parking is available at the 270 State St Garage ($1.50/hour with stickers from the front desk). Free street parking is available nearby after 4pm.

Yes. MakeHaven has elevator access to all floors, ADA-compliant doorways and walkways, and restrooms with hand rails. If you encounter an accessibility issue, please report it via the link on our contact page.

Storage & Workshop Pricing

There are designated drying areas and in-progress shelving available in most zones. Month-to-month bin and shelf storage is also available for a small additional fee.

Workshop pricing is calculated as: (Instructor Pay + MakeHaven Costs) ÷ Minimum Attendees + Supply Cost. Members receive discounted pricing on most workshops.
