Work at MakeHaven

Turn your passion for making into a purpose. Join our team and help empower the New Haven maker community.

Why Join Our Team?

Working at MakeHaven means surrounding yourself with creativity, innovation, and a community that loves to share knowledge. Whether you are teaching, facilitating, or managing, you are building the future of making in New Haven.

Space Access

Most staff and core volunteer positions include 24/7 access to our 10,000 sq. ft. facility and all its resources.

Vibrant Community

Work alongside 800+ passionate makers, engineers, artists, and entrepreneurs who are always eager to collaborate.

Skill Building

Constant opportunities for professional development, cross-training on new equipment, and learning from experts.
