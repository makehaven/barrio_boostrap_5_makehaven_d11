Programming & Events

Workshops, classes, and programs for every skill level.

From youth programs to professional certifications, MakeHaven runs programming year-round to help our community learn, grow, and connect.

A free introduction to making for New Haven-area youth. GEMS covers digital fabrication, woodworking, electronics, textiles, and more over a multi-week cohort.

  • Cohort-based, earn a certificate
