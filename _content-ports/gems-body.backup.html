Program Overview

Are you looking for a little extra structure to help you dive into MakeHaven?

Join the GEMS program, where you’ll earn digital badges—micro-credentials that showcase your safety and tool skills—while creating projects with your new abilities. Alongside 8-9 other participants and your GEMS Coach you’ll dedicate two evenings a week to hands-on making in our makerspace. You’ll cover wood, textiles, digital, and metal— all the basics to get you started at MakeHaven!

Over 10 weeks, you’ll gain hands-on experience through guided projects while earning badges that certify your tool proficiency. Build confidence and independence in the makerspace, and by the end, you’ll have completed four projects and become part of a supportive community of makers.

Why Join GEMS?

🛡️ Earn Digital Badges: Show off your skills with micro-credentials recognized at MakeHaven.

🛠️ Hands-On Learning: Dive into practical projects and gain experience with cutting-edge tools.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Guided by Experts: Work with your GEMS Coach, and skilled volunteers who will guide you every step of the way.

🎨 Create Portfolio-Worthy Projects: Complete and showcase a project in each focus area—perfect for building your maker portfolio.

💡 Skills for the Future: The confidence and abilities you gain will empower you to tackle personal projects, explore advanced workshops, or even pursue maker-related careers.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • ❓ Who can join the GEMS program? GEMS is designed for new members looking to dive into the maker community, but established members are also welcome to join and explore.
  • 🔧 Do I need prior experience with tools or fabrication? No prior experience is needed! GEMS is an introductory program perfect for beginners who want to get hands-on and start learning.
  • 📅 What happens if I miss a session? We’ll do our best to help you catch up, but it’s always better to let us know in advance if you can’t make it. Please note that instructors and coaches may have limited availability outside of scheduled hours.
  • 🛠️ Are materials provided, or do I need to bring my own? Your coach will provide recommendations for low-cost materials, and MakeHaven often has scraps available for small projects.
  • 📋 Can I join if I’m not a MakeHaven member? A MakeHaven membership is required to participate in GEMS, as the program is centered around using the space and earning badges.
  • 🏅 What are badges? Badges are micro-credentials that demonstrate your skills and are recognized within MakeHaven. They allow you to access tools and equipment independently once earned.

If you have any questions, please reach out to us at GEMS@MakeHaven.org.
