Our Mission

Equip people with tools to design, invent, and create.

Our Vision

The New Haven area is enriched by creativity and entrepreneurship.

MakeHaven is a not-for-profit membership organization for makers, creators, tinkerers and dreamers. We have the tools and instructors to support a wide variety of projects, including: prototyping, fabrication, electronics, crafts, art, woodworking and much more. Through free public events, skill-building workshops, and the independent projects of our membership, MakeHaven enables people to make things.

Our facility in the arts district of downtown New Haven contains an electronics shop, wood shop, metal shop, media lab, crafting and sewing area, 3D printing area, laser cutters, radio area, plastics and casting area, bike repair shop, and kitchen. All members are welcome to use our many resources.

Guiding Principles

Community

We practice collaboration and knowledge-sharing as keys to success.

We recognize that our community is stronger when everyone is involved and represented.

Optimism

Creativity

Activities Include

  • 24/7 access to the makerspace and all tools
  • Free public events and meetups on new technologies
  • Classes and workshops to build skills in art and technology
  • Sliding-scale membership for those with limited incomes
  • Volunteer coaching and project mentorship for members
  • Resources to assist aspiring entrepreneurs with prototypes
