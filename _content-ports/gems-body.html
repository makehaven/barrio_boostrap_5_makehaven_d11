GEMS: MakeHaven Intro Program

Explore, Create, Connect—Your Maker Journey Starts Here.

Program Overview

Are you looking for a little extra structure to help you dive into MakeHaven?

Join the GEMS program, where you'll earn digital badges—micro-credentials that showcase your safety and tool skills—while creating projects with your new abilities. Alongside 8-9 other participants and your GEMS Coach you'll dedicate two evenings a week to hands-on making in our makerspace. You'll cover wood, textiles, digital, and metal—all the basics to get you started!

Over 10 weeks, you'll gain hands-on experience through guided projects while earning badges that certify your tool proficiency. Build confidence and independence in the makerspace, and by the end, you'll have completed four projects and become part of a supportive community of makers.

Why Join GEMS?

Earn Digital Badges

Show off your skills with micro-credentials recognized at MakeHaven.

Hands-On Learning

Dive into practical projects and gain experience with cutting-edge tools.

Guided by Experts

Work with your GEMS Coach, and skilled volunteers who will guide you every step of the way.

Create Portfolio-Worthy Projects

Complete and showcase a project in each focus area—perfect for building your maker portfolio.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the GEMS program?

GEMS is designed for new members looking to dive into the maker community, but established members are also welcome to join and explore.

Do I need prior experience with tools?

No prior experience is needed! GEMS is an introductory program perfect for beginners who want to get hands-on.

Can I join if I'm not a MakeHaven member?

A MakeHaven membership is required to participate in GEMS, as the program is centered around using the space and earning badges.

Are materials provided?

Your coach will provide recommendations for low-cost materials, and MakeHaven often has scraps available for small projects.
