Unlock access to tools, workshops, and a vibrant network of makers. Whether you're joining for yourself or sharing the experience with someone else, here's how to get started.

Membership Includes

  • 24/7 keycard access to the makerspace
  • Use of all tools and machines
  • Volunteer-led tool orientations (free)
  • Discounted workshops and classes
  • Access to member-only events
  • Private studio workspace available
  • Community of makers, mentors & collaborators
  • Sliding-scale pricing available
